This app adds London Transit Commission buses information to MonTransit.

This app provides the buses schedule (offline) as well as detours, service changes and news from www.londontransit.ca and @LTCLdnOnt on Twitter.

LTC buses serve London in Ontario, Canada.

Once this application is installed, the MonTransit app will display buses information (schedule...).

This application only has a temporary icon: download the MonTransit app (free) in the "More ..." section bellow or by following this Google Play link https://goo.gl/pCk5mV

You can install this application on the SD card but it is not recommended.

The information comes from the GTFS file provided by the London Transit Commission (LTC).

This application is free and open-source:
https://github.com/mtransitapps/ca-london-transit-bus-android

This app is not related with the London Transit Commission (LTC).
